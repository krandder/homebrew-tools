## Starting test evidence

The repository contained four shell suites. A fresh sequential run found two
failures:

1. `test-ai-vault-http-sync.sh` accidentally executed the installed
   `ai-token` instead of the repository artifact and no longer observed the
   authority-specific failure after broker refresh was centralized.
2. `test-claude-token-owner-safety.sh` reached `ai-vault sync-receive`, whose
   dispatch read `$2` after command shifting even though the profile was `$1`.

The other two suites passed. The harness dependency was made hermetic and the
dispatch bug received a permanent regression check before the baseline was
declared green.

## Packaging drift

Fresh SHA-256 comparison found both `Formula/ai-vault.rb` and
`Formula/ai-vault-http.rb` out of sync with their source artifacts. A blocking
formula-integrity test now covers all three shipped artifacts.

## Green baseline after first TDD slice

`tests/run full` passed with 13 Python tests plus all four shell suites. The
new coverage includes formula integrity; owner/vault refresh isolation;
permanent versus transient refresh failure; atomic successful rotation;
follower sentinel publication; concurrent one-refresh serialization; and
plain-receive stale, conflicting, malformed, unversioned, and sentinel-write
rejection.
